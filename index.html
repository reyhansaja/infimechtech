What is CFD?

Read More
Computational Fluid Dynamics simulation, commonly known as CFD simulation, is one of the implementations of Computer Aided Engineering (CAE) that utilizes a numerical method approach with the assistance of computers for computational calculations. CFD simulation is widely used in the field of education and the industrial sector to assist engineers and researchers in understanding design and research outcomes.

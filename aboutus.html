About Us

This is Infimech

Infimech specializes in engineering consulting services utilizing numerical methods, CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics). We have been experienced since 2019 in handling various industrial projects, both domestically and internationally.

Read More

The R&D expenditure in Indonesia is low.

Starting from the founders' concern about Indonesia's lagging R&D figures, data from SJC (Scimago, 2021) indicates that Indonesia ranks 45th below neighboring countries such as Malaysia, Thailand, and Singapore. (Julia, 2015) In her research, she concluded that the biggest hindrance to R&D progress is the high research cost and the time wasted on trial and error analysis. These two factors often burden the research process and slow down the outcomes.

In order to streamline both time and costs, we have initiated a new breakthrough for researchers and product developers to accelerate the pace of research by employing numerical analysis and computational methods.

In addition to expediting analysis, this method is also cost-effective. By leveraging CAE (Computer Aided Engineering), research objects can be transformed into digital forms. This enables the phenomena on the object to be calculated using CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics) analysis, based on digital data. Consequently, modifications can be made easily, and the results of the research object can be observed. With the solutions we offer, we aim to advance research productivity and elevate R&D in Indonesia to become a leading R&D country on the global stage.

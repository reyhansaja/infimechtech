FEM Simulation Services

Do You Need Expertise to Handle Your Project or Research?

We provide simulation services related to fluid flow and heat transfer using the finite element method (FEM). FEM can be a solution to your challenges. By leveraging numerical methods supported by computational models, FEM simulation can address your material and design issues.

Infimech provides FEM simulation services and data processing solutions. We are equipped to handle a wide range of research projects in both academic and industrial backgrounds. The outcomes of our FEM simulations may include quantitative data, graphs, images, and animated videos, supplemented by comprehensive discussions, conclusions, and recommendations derived from the conducted research or design. Thus, with FEM, users can efficiently, accurately, and cost-effectively address engineering challenges.
